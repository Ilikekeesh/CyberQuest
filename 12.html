Lesson 1.2: Common Cyber Threats

Understanding cyber threats is crucial to protect yourself and your organization. Click each section to learn more:

Malware is malicious software that damages or gains unauthorized access to systems. Examples: viruses, worms, trojans.

Phishing tricks users into revealing sensitive info via fake emails or websites.

Ransomware encrypts files and demands payment for decryption.

Distributed Denial-of-Service attacks overwhelm systems, making them unavailable.

Threats from individuals within an organization who misuse access privileges.
